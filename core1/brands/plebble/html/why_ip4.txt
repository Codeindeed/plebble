Why IPv4 Addresses?
===================

As opposed to the newest version 6 of the Internet Protocol, version 4 (IPv4) is a stable protocol with peculiar characteristics that can be exploited to achieve consensus in a BFT problem [Search Byzantine Fault Tolerant].
